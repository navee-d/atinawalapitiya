About the Program

The Higher National Diploma in Management (HNDM) equips students with essential business, leadership, and managerial skills, emphasizing practical projects and industry readiness.

The curriculum covers management principles, finance, marketing, human resources, project management, and professional practice, preparing graduates for employment or further studies.

Curriculum Structure

Year 1 - Foundation

Core Business Fundamentals

Introduction to management, accounting basics, and business communication.

  • Principles of Management
  • Business Mathematics
  • Business Communication

Year 2 - Advanced

Applied Management Skills

Developing decision-making, managerial competence, and business expertise.

  • Human Resource Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Law & Ethics

Year 3 - Specialization

Projects & Internship

Capstone project and industrial training to apply knowledge in real-world settings.

  • Strategic Management
  • Project Management
  • Industrial Training (6 months)
  • Leadership & Professional Practice
