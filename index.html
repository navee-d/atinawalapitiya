About ATI Nawalapitiya

ATI Nawalapitiya, established under the Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education (SLIATE) in 1995, stands as a beacon of technical education in the Central Province. Our institute is committed to "Creating Excellent Higher National Diplomates with Modern Technology for Sustainable Development."

Located in the scenic town of Nawalapitiya, our campus provides an ideal learning environment that combines academic excellence with practical training. We are dedicated to fostering skilled professionals who can contribute meaningfully to Sri Lanka's economic development.

Under the Ministry of Higher Education, ATI Nawalapitiya has been serving the community for nearly three decades, producing graduates who excel in tourism, management, English education, and information technology sectors.

Our Programs

HND in Tourism and Hospitality Management

HNDTHM

Prepare for a dynamic career in the tourism and hospitality industry with comprehensive practical training and industry exposure.

  • Duration: 2-3 years
  • NVQ Level 6 equivalent
  • Hotel management training
  • Tourism planning expertise
  • Industry internships

HND in Management

HNDM

Develop essential leadership and management skills for business success in the modern corporate environment.

  • Duration: 2-3 years
  • Business leadership focus
  • Strategic management
  • Financial management
  • Human resource management

HND in English

HND English

Enhance your language proficiency and communication skills for teaching and professional roles in the global marketplace.

  • Duration: 2-3 years
  • Advanced language skills
  • Teaching methodology
  • Literature studies
  • Professional communication

HND in Science Laboratory Technology

HNDSLT

Gain hands-on experience in laboratory techniques and scientific research for careers in science and industry.

  • Duration: 2-3 years
  • Laboratory safety & management
  • Analytical instrumentation
  • Research methodology
  • Internship opportunities

HND in Information Technology

HNDIT

Build a strong foundation in information technology and software development for the digital age.

  • Duration: 2-3 years
  • NVQ Level 6 equivalent
  • Software development
  • Database management
  • Network administration
  • Web development
  • System analysis & design

Admissions

Entry Requirements

  • G.C.E. Advanced Level qualification in any stream
  • Pass in English (O/L)
  • Pass in Mathematics (O/L)
  • Aptitude test (where applicable)
  • Interview assessment

Application Process

  • Online application via apply.sliate.ac.lk
  • Submit required documents
  • Pay application fee
  • Attend aptitude test/interview
  • Await selection results

Fee Structure

Our programs are designed to be affordable while maintaining high quality education standards.

  • Registration Fee: Rs. 5,000
  • Semester Fee: Rs. 12,000 - 30,000
  • Examination Fee: Rs. 2,500
  • Library Fee: Rs. 1,000

*Fees are subject to change. Please contact the institute for the latest fee structure.
